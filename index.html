Whirlpool of Doom

My project is an app used for mainly college students and many more people. My program is written in JavaScript using the App Lab programming environment in Code Studio. Usually some people have a hard time depicting what their dorm would look like. The purpose of my program is to have people able to have somewhat a virtual tour of their dorm/room and decide which furniture they would like to put in the room, and also decide which room they would prefer. This way, the user can have an interactive way to viewing their dorm without having to worry about what their dorm would look like and what furniture to buy. They would be able to decide what their dorm would look like by clicking on the small pictures of the furniture, and decide which one they like better.
